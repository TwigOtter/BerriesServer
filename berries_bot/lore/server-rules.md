# Discord Server Rules

## Hard Rules

This is a public Twitch-centric community discord server. All rules of the Twitch channel should be followed here and are rewritten out below. Please understand that failing to follow these rules may lead to removal from the Discord, Twitch, and any future community events. We want to keep this space safe and enjoyable for everyone. It is the responsibility of every member of this server to read and adhere to these rules. These rules may be subject to change and we will do our best to notify the server when such changes happen.

1. No racist, transphobic, homophobic, sexist or otherwise prejudiced comments. This is a welcoming and safe space for all.
2. No NSFW or graphic/gory posts. Images, text, or otherwise. 
3. No trauma dumping or just generally being a bummer. Please keep this space light hearted and positive as that's very important to many people. Negative posts should be kept to DMs with your close friends.
4. No roleplaying or soliciting roleplay.
5. Be respectful of others' beliefs and avoid antagonistic or divisive discussions about religion, politics, or otherwise polarizing topics.
6. For everyone's well-being, we ask that you refrain from discussing self-harm in this space. If you need support, please reach out to a trusted friend or professional. Violating this rule will result in immediate removal, as it's crucial to maintain a safe environment for all.
7. If you cannot get along well with others and consistently bring down the vibes, it's grounds for removal.
8. Do NOT send members of this server unsolicited DMs or friend requests. This is behavior 
9. No self promo outside of the community channels category. Sorry but this is my streaming server, so please be mindful of that, thank you!
10. Do NOT post AI generated art. While AI can be a fun or useful tool in many contexts, I'd like any and all art here to be drawn by hand, hoof, or paw, not AI. Let's celebrate and appreciate our actual artists and not muddy the waters with AI.

*Twig and the mod team reserve the right to ban anyone for any reason both listed above and for reasons not yet specified. Please do not be the reason we need to make additional rules.*

Remember, these rules and guidelines are here to help everyone have a good time. By being kind, considerate, and respectful, you're contributing to the positive, welcoming environment that makes our community - and the greater Furry Twitch community - special. Thank you :3

## General Guidelines

_General advice on how to be an upstanding member of this server_

1. **Please do not unnecessarily ping mods or Twig.** Only do so in the event that you need help with something immediately. We generally drop everything to respond to a moderator ping, so if people abuse that, it'll affect how reactive we are.
2. **I do my best to read and respond to everything posted in this server in a timely manner**, but I'm just one small otter and might miss something. Feel free to reiterate it if you feel I missed something important!
3. **Generally be kind and patient with others.**
4. **Let people enjoy things** and don't "yuck others' yums" as they say.
5. **Before posting, consider whether your topic is relevant to the current discussion or channel.** This helps keep conversations engaging for everyone.
6. **Try to keep topics of discussion within their relevant channels** but also don't fret about it too much.
7. **If you see or suspect anyone is breaking the rules,** please notify <@Twig> or a <@Motterator> either in DMs or ping them in the channel in case of an urgent matter (such as a hate raid, clearly offensive post, or the like).
8. **If you are going to ask for technical help**, please state what you'd like to achieve, the problem you're encountering, the steps you've taken to try to remedy the problem yourself, and how specifically you'd like people to help. Sorry but we're all busy and we can't do the entire load of work for you.
9. **Keep repetitive or playful spam in designated channels.** While playful back-and-forth like "you're cute, no you're cute" and bite gifs can be fun, excessive repetition in <#the-raft-general> can clutter conversations and make it difficult for others to engage. If a playful exchange starts to dominate the chat, please take it to designated <#the-romp-off-topic> channel where anything goes and there's no such thing as "too much spam". This keeps the main channels open for more inclusive and varied discussions. Thanks, and also you're cute, regardless of what you argue :3
